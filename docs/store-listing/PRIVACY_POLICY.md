# Privacy Policy — PDF Master

**Last updated:** 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the **PDF Master** mobile app ("PDF Master", "the app",
"we", "us") handles information. PDF Master is designed to be private by default: you do
not need an account, and your documents are processed on your device.

> **Hosted at:** https://kavin511.github.io/PdfMaster/privacy-policy.html — paste this URL
> into Play Console → App content → Privacy policy. (Served from `docs/privacy-policy.html`
> via GitHub Pages.)

---

## Summary (the short version)

- **No account.** You can use PDF Master without signing up or giving us personal details.
- **Your documents stay on your device.** PDFs and images you open, scan, edit, sign or
  annotate are processed locally. We do **not** upload them to any server — in fact, we
  operate no servers that receive your files.
- **Analytics is optional and off by default.** If you opt in, we collect anonymous usage
  and performance data only — never the contents, names, or metadata of your documents.
- **Purchases** are handled by Google Play Billing; we never see your payment details.

---

## 1. Information we process

### a) Documents and files — processed on your device
When you open, scan, import, edit, sign, annotate, merge, split, compress, protect, unlock,
or run text recognition (OCR) on a file, all processing happens **locally on your device**.
Your files, their contents, and their names are **not** transmitted to us or to any third
party by the app. Files you create or edit are saved to the location you choose on your
device.

### b) Camera — for scanning only
With your permission, the app uses your camera to scan documents. Captured images are
processed on-device and saved as PDFs locally. Camera images are not sent to us.

### c) Optional, anonymous analytics
If — and only if — you opt in (you are asked during onboarding, and can change it any time
in **Settings → Share usage analytics**), the app uses **Google Firebase Analytics** to
collect anonymous information that helps us improve the app, such as:

- which features are used (e.g. "merge", "scan", "annotate", "compress") and general
  in-app navigation;
- aggregate app performance and stability signals;
- whether the device has a premium subscription (as a non-identifying property).

We do **not** collect the contents of your documents, file names, or any personal
information through analytics. Analytics is **disabled by default**; if you do not opt in,
no analytics data is collected.

### d) Purchases and subscriptions
In-app purchases and subscriptions (PDF Master Pro) are processed by **Google Play
Billing**. Google handles your payment and billing information; we do not receive or store
your payment-card or personal payment details. The app stores locally only whether your
device currently has an active entitlement.

### e) Biometric app lock (optional)
If you enable the optional app lock, biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) is handled
entirely by your device's operating system. We never receive, see, or store your biometric
data.

---

## 2. Permissions and why we use them

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| Camera | Scan physical documents into PDFs (on-device) |
| Photos / media (read, user-selected) | Let you pick images to convert into PDFs |
| Storage (read/write) | Open the PDFs you choose and save your results |
| Internet & network state | Enable in-app purchases and, if you opt in, send anonymous analytics |
| Biometric | Optional app lock, handled by your device's OS |
| Vibrate | Haptic feedback in the interface |

You can revoke permissions at any time in your device's system settings.

---

## 3. Third-party services

PDF Master relies on the following Google services, governed by Google's own privacy
policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy):

- **Google Play Billing / Google Play services** — for purchases and subscriptions.
- **Google Firebase Analytics** — only if you opt in to analytics.

PDF Master does **not** currently display third-party advertising and does not include
advertising SDKs. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated and the
app's Play Data safety section will reflect it.

---

## 4. Data retention and deletion

- **Documents:** remain on your device under your control. Deleting a file in the app or
  via your device removes it. Uninstalling the app removes its locally stored data.
- **Analytics:** you can turn analytics off any time in Settings; we stop collecting it.
  Aggregate analytics already collected are retained by Firebase per Google's policies.
- **Cache:** you can clear the app's cache from **Settings → Clear cache**.

---

## 5. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA and similar)

Because the app is accountless and does not collect personal identifiers, we hold no
personal profile to export or delete. You can:

- opt in or out of anonymous analytics at any time in Settings;
- control all app permissions through your device settings;
- remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the app.

If you are in the EEA/UK or California, you may also have rights under the GDPR or CCPA. For
any request or question, contact us (below).

---

## 6. Children's privacy

PDF Master is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed to children under 13
(or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children.

---

## 7. Security

Because your documents are processed on-device and are not uploaded to us, they are subject
to your device's own security. We recommend using a device lock and the app's optional
biometric lock for sensitive files.
