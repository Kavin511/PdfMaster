## Full description (max 4000 chars)

PDF Master helps you view, edit, scan, sign and manage PDF documents on your phone — all in one place. It is designed to be simple, fast and private, with no account required to get started.

**View and read**
Open and read PDFs with smooth zoom, page navigation and in-document search.

**Edit and organize**
Add or edit text inside a PDF, insert images, and rotate, delete, reorder or duplicate pages. Your documents stay sharp because pages keep their original text instead of being converted to images.

**Scan documents**
Use your camera to scan documents, receipts and notes. Automatic edge detection helps capture clean, straight pages that you can save directly as a PDF.

**Sign and fill forms**
Create a signature and place it on contracts and agreements. Fill in interactive PDF forms and save your changes.

**Annotate**
Highlight, underline, strikethrough and draw on your documents. Add notes, shapes, arrows and stamps, then save them into the PDF.

**Merge and split**
Combine several PDFs into one document, or extract the specific pages you need into a new file.

**Compress and convert**
Reduce the size of large PDFs to make them easier to share, and convert images into a PDF document.

**Protect and unlock**
Add a password to protect sensitive files, remove passwords from documents you own, and redact content you want to keep private.

**Text recognition and export**
Recognize text in scanned documents and export your PDF to editable Word or text formats.

**Manage your files**
Browse all your documents, find recent files quickly, and mark favorites. Sort your files and switch between list and grid views.

**PDF Master Pro**
PDF Master is free to use for viewing, scanning and core tools. PDF Master Pro unlocks the full set of features, including text editing, signatures, text recognition, annotation, redaction, form filling, export to Word or text, and unlimited use of merge, split and compress, with no ads or watermark. Subscription details and pricing are shown in the app before you purchase.

We'd love your feedback. If you have a question or a feature request, please get in touch through the app.
