## Full description (max 4000 chars)

**PDF Master is the only PDF app you'll ever need.** 📄 Scan, edit, sign, annotate, merge, split, compress and protect your documents — all from one fast, beautifully simple app. No account. No sign-up. Just open and go.

Whether you're a student marking up lecture notes, a professional signing contracts, or just trying to shrink a file small enough to email, PDF Master does it in seconds.

✍️ **EDIT LIKE A PRO**
Add, replace and restyle text directly inside any PDF. Insert images, rotate and delete pages, and rearrange your document exactly how you want it — without flattening your file into a blurry image.

📷 **SCAN ANYTHING**
Turn your phone into a pocket scanner. Capture documents, receipts and whiteboards with automatic edge detection and crisp, clean output. Save straight to PDF.

🖊️ **SIGN & FILL FORMS**
Create your signature once, then drop it on any contract, invoice or agreement. Fill in interactive PDF forms and save them — no printing, no scanning.

🖍️ **ANNOTATE EVERYTHING**
Highlight, underline, strikethrough, draw freehand, add sticky notes, shapes, arrows and stamps (Approved, Confidential, Draft and more). Mark up documents and save your notes right into the PDF.

🔀 **MERGE & SPLIT**
Combine multiple PDFs into one, or pull out exactly the pages you need. Reorder, duplicate and delete pages with a simple drag.

🗜️ **COMPRESS & CONVERT**
Shrink huge PDFs down to email-friendly sizes while keeping them sharp. Convert your photos into a single polished PDF in one tap.

🔒 **PROTECT & UNLOCK**
Lock sensitive documents with a password, or remove passwords from files you own. Permanently redact confidential text so it can never be recovered.

🔍 **OCR & EXPORT**
Extract text from scanned pages with smart text recognition, then export your PDF to editable Word or plain text.

📁 **STAY ORGANIZED**
Browse all your files, jump back into recent documents, and star your favorites. Search, sort and switch between grid and list views instantly.

— WHY PDF MASTER —
✓ All-in-one: every PDF tool in a single app
✓ Private by design: no account, no cloud upload required
✓ Fast and lightweight
✓ Clean, modern, easy-to-use interface
✓ Keeps your text sharp — never rasterizes your pages

⭐ **PDF MASTER PRO**
Unlock the full suite — text editing, signatures, OCR, annotation, redaction, form filling, Office export, unlimited merge/split/compress, and an ad-free, watermark-free experience.

Download PDF Master today and take control of every document in your pocket.
