Privacy Policy — PDF Master

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the PDF Master mobile app ("PDF Master", "the app", "we", "us") handles information. PDF Master is designed to be private by default: you do not need an account, and your documents are processed on your device.

The short version
  • No account. Use PDF Master without signing up.
  • Your documents stay on your device. We operate no servers that receive your files; nothing is uploaded.
  • Analytics is optional and off by default. If you opt in, we collect anonymous usage data only — never document contents, names, or metadata.
  • Purchases are handled by Google Play Billing; we never see your payment details.

1. Information we process

a) Documents and files — processed on your device

When you open, scan, import, edit, sign, annotate, merge, split, compress, protect, unlock, or run text recognition (OCR) on a file, all processing happens locally on your device. Your files, their contents, and their names are not transmitted to us or any third party by the app. Files you create are saved to the location you choose on your device.

b) Camera — for scanning only

With your permission, the app uses your camera to scan documents. Captured images are processed on-device and saved as PDFs locally. Camera images are not sent to us.

c) Optional, anonymous analytics

If — and only if — you opt in (you are asked during onboarding, and can change it any time in Settings → Share usage analytics), the app uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous information that helps us improve the app, such as which features are used (e.g. "merge", "scan", "annotate"), general in-app navigation, aggregate performance and stability signals, and whether the device has a premium subscription (as a non-identifying property).

We do not collect the contents of your documents, file names, or any personal information through analytics. Analytics is disabled by default; if you do not opt in, no analytics data is collected.

d) Purchases and subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions (PDF Master Pro) are processed by Google Play Billing. Google handles your payment and billing information; we do not receive or store your payment details. The app stores locally only whether your device currently has an active entitlement.

e) Biometric app lock (optional)

If you enable the optional app lock, biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We never receive, see, or store your biometric data.

3. Third-party services

PDF Master relies on the following Google services, governed by Google's privacy policy:

  • Google Play Billing / Google Play services — for purchases and subscriptions.
  • Google Firebase Analytics — only if you opt in to analytics.

PDF Master does not currently display third-party advertising and does not include advertising SDKs. If this changes in a future version, this policy and the app's Play Data safety section will be updated.

4. Data retention and deletion

  • Documents: remain on your device under your control. Deleting a file, or uninstalling the app, removes the relevant data.
  • Analytics: turn it off any time in Settings; we stop collecting it. Aggregate analytics already collected are retained by Firebase per Google's policies.
  • Cache: clear it from Settings → Clear cache.

5. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA and similar)

Because the app is accountless and does not collect personal identifiers, we hold no personal profile to export or delete. You can opt in/out of anonymous analytics in Settings, control permissions in your device settings, and remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the app. EEA/UK and California residents may have additional rights under the GDPR or CCPA; contact us for any request.

6. Children's privacy

PDF Master is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

Because your documents are processed on-device and are not uploaded to us, they are subject to your device's own security. We recommend using a device lock and the app's optional biometric lock for sensitive files.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, through an in-app notice.
