## 3. ASO keyword strategy

Play ranks primarily on the **title** and **descriptions** (no separate keyword field). Weave
these naturally (already reflected above) — do **not** stuff or repeat:

- Primary: `pdf editor`, `pdf scanner`, `pdf reader`
- Secondary: `merge pdf`, `split pdf`, `compress pdf`, `sign pdf`, `fill pdf forms`, `scan to pdf`
- Long-tail: `edit pdf text`, `images to pdf`, `password protect pdf`, `offline pdf editor`

Rules followed: each key term appears in context, no hidden text, no competitor/brand terms,
no "free" spam, claims match actual functionality.

## 5. Data safety form (REQUIRED — fill exactly)

The app collects anonymous analytics by default (user can opt out in Settings → Privacy), so the
form **must** declare it. Suggested answers:

**Does your app collect or share user data?** → **Yes**

| Data type | Collected? | Shared? | Purpose | Optional? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| App activity → App interactions | Yes | No | Analytics, App functionality | Yes (opt-out in Settings) |
| App info & performance → Crash logs / Diagnostics *(only if Crashlytics is enabled)* | Yes | No | Analytics, App functionality | Yes |

- **Is all collected data encrypted in transit?** → Yes
- **Can users request data deletion?** → Yes (provide the support email / privacy-policy method)
- **Is data used for tracking / advertising?** → **No**
- **Linked to a user's identity?** → **No** (no account, no user-id is set)

> Files, documents, scans, and their contents are processed **on-device only** and are **not**
> collected or transmitted — do **not** declare "Files and docs" as collected.

## 6. Permissions — declared & justified (for the listing + review)

| Permission | Why | Shown to user |
|---|---|---|
| CAMERA | Document scanning | Requested at first scan, with rationale |
| READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / photo picker | Pick images for Image→PDF | At point of use |
| (Storage) Scoped Storage / SAF | Open & save PDFs the user chooses | System file picker |

No broad `MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`, no location, no contacts, no background permissions.

## 7. Subscriptions disclosure (Play Subscriptions policy)

The store listing and the in-app paywall must clearly state, before purchase:
- Price and billing period of each plan (Weekly / Annual / Lifetime).
- Free-trial length and that it converts to a paid subscription unless cancelled.
- That subscriptions auto-renew until cancelled, and how to cancel (Play subscriptions).
- Links to Terms and Privacy Policy (already linked from the paywall).

The in-app `PremiumScreen` already shows live localized prices, a "Cancel anytime" note, and
Terms/Privacy links — keep these accurate.
